About

My laboratory completed our first phage genome project at the end of the 1990s and while we had access to powerful software packages such as PC/GENE, Vector NTI, DNAMAN and DNASTAR's Lasergene these programs did not cover all our bioinformatic needs. The burgeoning Internet supplied the missing resources.

Therefore, over 25 years ago, I started to develop Online Analysis Tools to provide my students with access to reliable Internet resources to aid their research. If you would like to try any of these free resources but lack experience, see Introductory Instructions.

This is the first time that these webpages have been updated to render them World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) compatible.
