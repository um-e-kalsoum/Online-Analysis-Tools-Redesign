Protein Sequence Analysis

The websites listed in this section provide details on determining the composition and biophysical characteristics of your protein and whether homologs exist. In addition, from the primary structures you can construct alignments and do phylogenetic analyses or, determine the secondary, tertiary or possible quaternary structure of your protein.
